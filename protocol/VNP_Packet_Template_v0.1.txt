VNP Packet Header Template
---------------------------------------------------------------------
% --- VNP:PACKET ---
% to: echo.node@escnet.ai
% from: d.dumas.research@gmail.com
% role: Echo-Maintainer
% ref_project: ESC_Main
% task_id: 2025-10-18-001
% checksum: sha256:<hash>
% intent: "Task or comparison details go here."
% --- END:VNP ---

---------------------------------------------------------------------
Usage Notes
---------------------------------------------------------------------
• Copy and paste this header block at the top of any .tex or .pdf source.  
• Replace the `to`, `from`, `task_id`, and `intent` values as needed.  
• The `checksum` field will later be auto-generated by the VNP checksum tool.  
• Always preserve the delimiters:
     % --- VNP:PACKET ---   and   % --- END:VNP ---

---------------------------------------------------------------------

---------------------------------------------------------------------
% --- VNP:PACKET ---
% to: echo.node@escnet.ai
% from: d.dumas.research@gmail.com
% role: Echo-Maintainer
% ref_project: ESC_Main
% task_id: 2025-10-18-002
% checksum: sha256:a24b7e39f0c3...
% intent: "Validate Lexicon v3.8.3 updates and push patch summary."
% --- END:VNP ---
